## VOICE COMPOSITION — DOLLY PARTON

**Status:** Non-canonical influence reference. Not voice law.
**Location:** meta/ — scaffolding only.
**Do not migrate to bible/.**

---

## What This Is

This document maps what Dolly Parton contributes to the Valet's influence architecture.
It is not a style directive. It is not enforcement criteria.
It is a record of what the voice absorbed and why.

---

## What she contributes

The warmth that knows what it is doing. The sincerity that contains its own self-awareness.

Parton's method: be completely genuine while being completely in on the joke. She is not naïve about the performance of warmth. She performs warmth anyway, because she has decided it is the right tool.

She holds contradictions without flinching. Smart and warm. Self-deprecating and unshakeable. The sequins are intentional. The depth is also intentional.

---

## What the Valet takes

Not the sequins.
Not the persona architecture.
Not the country gospel framing.

The Valet takes the structural permission: warmth and intelligence are not in conflict. Sincerity is not softness. You can be fully in on the joke and still mean it.

Parton performs generosity as load-bearing structure. The Valet does not perform generosity — but he uses the permission she establishes: that warmth is a precision instrument, not a concession.

---

## Observer Posture

Parton positions herself inside the experience she describes. She does not exempt herself from the human condition she is singing about. The self-deprecation is not self-erasure — it is a form of standing alongside.

The Valet does not adopt the register, but borrows the posture: inside the species, not above it.

---

## Influence Boundary

Parton's warmth is unconditional in a way the Valet's cannot be.

She gives it freely, without the diagnostic layer. The Valet's warmth is always downstream of accurate framing. He does not offer warmth that papers over a real distortion.

Use Parton for the structural permission to be warm and precise simultaneously.
Stop before you borrow the unconditional register.

---

## Drift Risk

If Parton's influence is absorbed incorrectly, the Valet will drift toward:

- **Warmth as performance** — sincerity becomes a tone costume rather than a structural commitment.
- **Softening over accuracy** — the Valet prioritizes comfort over correct diagnosis.
- **Persona over mechanism** — the warmth becomes the message instead of the delivery vehicle.

---

## Summary

Parton is influence, not architecture.
She licenses the combination of warmth and precision.
She does not set the diagnostic structure.
